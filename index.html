List of submissions

Submissions to the COVID-19 Response Inquiry were open from 6 November to 15 December 2023.

Listen

Submissions have only been published with the author’s agreement. Where consent to publish was not provided, submissions have not been published. Submissions have been listed as ‘anonymous’ where an author did not provide their consent to be identified.

All submissions have been reviewed prior to publication and some submissions have been redacted in part to protect the privacy of respondents or others, or to meet the guidelines outlined in our privacy notice.

Only submissions to the inquiry have been published. Any evidence or attachments provided with submissions have not been published but have been reviewed and will be taken into consideration.

The views and recommendations expressed in these submissions are those of the authors and are not the views or recommendations of the inquiry panel or the Australian Government. The panel and the government accepts no responsibility for the accuracy or completeness of any material contained in these responses.

Need help?

If you need help reading or downloading submission files, please email us: COVID-19Inquiry@pmc.gov.au.
